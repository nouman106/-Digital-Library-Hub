📖 Khidmat Ka Maqsad (How It Works)

KitabGhar ek muft online library hai jo ilm ko aam karne ke liye banayi gayi hai.

Hamare platform par aap in kitabon ko browser mein parh sakte hain:

  • 📕 Islami Kitabein — Din o Dunya ki rehbari ke liye
  • 🎓 Talimi Kitabein — Science, Math, History aur zyadah
  • 🔥 Hosla Afzai — Aapka jazba barhane ke liye
  • 🧒 Bachon Ki Kitabein — Chhoti umar mein bari taleem

⚙️ Admins Ke Liye (For Admins)

  • Sirf woh kitabein add karein jinka aapke paas haq hai (copyright clear ho)
  • Galat ya harmful content upload karna sakht mana hai
  • Admin password kisi se share na karein
  • Data ki zimmedari aap par hai — backup rakhein
  • IndexedDB mein data local device par rehta hai — server backup nahi hota
⚠️ Admin panel ka galat istimaal account ban ka sabab ban sakta hai.

📋 Content Policy

  1. Halal Content: Sirf woh content jo Islami wa qanuni usoolon ke mutabiq ho
  2. Copyright: Sirf woh kitabein add karein jo public domain mein hon ya aapke paas permission ho
  3. Quality: Clearly illegible ya corrupt PDF files na daalein
  4. Accuracy: Kitab ki sahi malumat (naam, author, category) fill karein
  5. Privacy: Kisi ki personal malumat share na karein

💾 Data & Privacy

KitabGhar mein aapka data sirf aapke browser mein store hota hai (IndexedDB). Hamare servers par koi data nahi jaata. Isliye:

  • Browser clear karne par data mit sakta hai — backup rakhein
  • Doosre device par data access nahi hoga
  • Hum aapki koi personal malumat nahi jante aur nahi rakhte
🔒 Aapki privacy hamari priority hai — koi tracking nahi, koi ads nahi.

⚠️ Disclaimer

KitabGhar mein maujood kitabein sirf taleem aur ilm ke maqsad ke liye hain. Kisi kitab ka content third-party hai — ham us content ki sahihat ki guarantee nahi dete. Parhnay wala khud zimmedaar hai.
