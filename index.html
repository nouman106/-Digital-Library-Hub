Our Story

📖 Digital Library Hub was born from a simple idea: to make quality Urdu and English e-books accessible to everyone, everywhere. We believe that knowledge should be free of barriers – whether geographical, financial, or technological.


🌟 What started as a small personal collection has grown into a curated platform with hundreds of titles in Islamic studies, children's literature, history, self-development, and more. Our mission is to empower readers and support authors by providing a beautiful, easy-to-use digital space.


✨ Today, thousands of readers trust us to deliver high-quality content with a seamless reading experience. And we're just getting started.

How It Works & Guidelines

📖 For Readers
• Browse the library, click any book.
• Free books: click "Read Now" – PDF opens instantly.
• Paid books: demo purchase (no real payment) – then read.
• No account needed – all books are stored in your browser.
🔧 For Admins
• Click "Admin Mode" → password: admin123.
• Add books with cover image, PDF, custom theme colors.
• Edit/delete books easily.
• All changes are saved locally.
📜 Content Policy
• Respect intellectual property – only upload books you own or have rights to.
• No offensive or illegal material.
• Admin may remove any book without notice.
